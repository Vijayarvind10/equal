Privacy & safety

Clear guardrails for every bit of data we hold

NeighborLift only keeps information needed to match volunteers, support residents, and deliver training. We treat every record as a trust.

  1. Minimum data, maximum security. We store contact details, scheduling preferences, and safety notes in encrypted systems with restricted access.
  2. No selling or sharing. We never sell data and we only share with partners when residents have given written consent.
  3. Control stays with you. Email privacy@neighborlift.org with “Delete my information” and we will confirm removal within 24 hours.
  4. Incident transparency. If any privacy issue occurs, we alert affected parties within one business day and publish a summary of what changed.

We maintain audits of story permissions, visit logs, and volunteer agreements so you can request a copy at any time.
