Workshop menu

Campus-ready sessions with clear operations

Each workshop includes learning objectives, a time-stamped agenda, slides or handouts, and facilitator notes so you can run it yourself or bring Equal Voices Collective to your class.

All offerings include a pre-call to gather context, optional co-facilitation with instructors, and follow-up notes within 24 hours.

Listening with care

Objectives

  • Build shared ground rules using community-origin language.
  • Practice layered listening exercises with debrief scripts.
  • Document commitments for future discussions.

Agenda

TimeSegment

Materials
