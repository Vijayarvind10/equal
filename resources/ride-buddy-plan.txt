Ride and Appointment Buddy Plan

60-SECOND OVERVIEW
Plan transport support for appointments, paperwork visits, and essential errands.
Ensure two volunteers per ride: driver and companion.

CHECKLIST
Confirm appointment details, duration, and waiting rules.
Print or screenshot directions, parking info, and entry requirements.
Bring a note page for follow ups and medication reminders.

DURING THE VISIT
Driver stays with the vehicle if required; companion tracks time and needs.
Log key questions and relay them to family or coordinators.

AFTERCARE
Schedule a check in call within 24 hours for medication pickup, rest, or next steps.
