Volunteer Daybook

PURPOSE
Track every visit, call, or errand in one place for easy reporting.

COLUMNS
Date and time, volunteer names, resident, task, supplies, mileage, notes, follow up, status.

USAGE TIPS
Color code by task type such as meals, transport, check in.
Add links to receipts or photos when sharing digitally.

WEEKLY REVIEW
Summarize wins, blockers, and escalations in the coordinator briefing.
