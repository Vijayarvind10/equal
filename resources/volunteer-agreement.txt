Volunteer Agreement and Orientation Notes

PURPOSE
Set expectations, safety standards, and confidentiality agreements for every volunteer.

COMMITMENTS
Arrive on time or notify the hotline.
Log visits in the daybook within 12 hours.
Respect resident privacy and consent choices.

ORIENTATION AGENDA
Welcome and mission overview.
Safety briefing and escalation walkthrough.
Shadow sign up and buddy pairing.

SIGN-OFF
Volunteer signature, coordinator signature, date.
