Peer Review Checklist with Gentle Language

60-SECOND OVERVIEW
Guide students through feedback that is specific, kind, and grounded in the text.

BEFORE YOU READ
Notice what the writer asked you to focus on and restate it in your own words.

CHECKLIST
1. Appreciate: name one move that supports the reader.
2. Clarify: point to a sentence or idea that needs more context.
3. Connect: suggest a source, example, or story that could deepen the point.
4. Care: confirm that consent and privacy agreements are honored.

CLOSING
Writers reply with one concrete next step and thank their partner.
