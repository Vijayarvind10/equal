One Hour Circle Plan: Gender and Everyday Rules

60-SECOND OVERVIEW
Explore how gendered expectations show up in homes, campuses, and workplaces.

TIMING
60 minutes

AGENDA
0:00-0:07 Welcome, consent reminders, and grounding breath.
0:07-0:22 Round 1: share an everyday rule you were taught about gender.
0:22-0:40 Round 2: read and discuss a story clip with optional audio.
0:40-0:55 Round 3: identify supports and repairs needed in your setting.
0:55-1:00 Gratitude, optional check-ins, and documentation of next steps.

MATERIALS
Story clips or transcripts
Notecards for commitments
Resource sheet for campus support
