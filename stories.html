Story guidelines

  • Your story belongs to you. We only edit for clarity or safety and always send a draft before posting.
  • You can withdraw or anonymize at any time.
  • No identifying details about residents without explicit permission.
  • Focus on what others can learn: tools used, timing, and tips for future circuits.
