Reflection Rubric for Identity Topics

60-SECOND OVERVIEW
Assess reflective writing with criteria that honor listening, consent, and thoughtful use of sources.

CRITERIA
Listening and care: 4 excellent when the writer cites peers respectfully and seeks consent before sharing details.
Use of course materials: 4 excellent when concepts are applied accurately with citations.
Personal insight: 4 excellent when the writer names growth and next steps with humility.
Craft and clarity: 4 excellent when sentences are clear, paced, and accessible.

SCORING GUIDANCE
3 proficient: meets expectations with minor notes for depth or clarity.
2 developing: needs coaching on grounding claims or honoring consent agreements.
1 emerging: revisit ground rules and invite a revision with support.
