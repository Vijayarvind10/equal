Quick Write Prompts and Exit Tickets

60-SECOND OVERVIEW
Use these prompts to open or close sessions on sensitive topics in under five minutes.

PROMPTS
1. What surprised you and how will you follow up with care?
2. Name a question you want to hold and one support you need.
3. Describe a moment where you listened differently today.
4. Which community agreement felt most useful and why?
5. What story do you want to document or revisit after class?

TIPS
Invite students to note whether you can quote their writing in future sessions.
