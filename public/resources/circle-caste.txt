One Hour Circle Plan: Caste, Class, and Education

60-SECOND OVERVIEW
Hold a consent-centered circle that links caste, class, and educational pathways.

TIMING
60 minutes

AGENDA
0:00-0:08 Arrival, breathing, consent script, and land acknowledgment.
0:08-0:20 Opening round: name one classroom moment about belonging or exclusion.
0:20-0:40 Story pairs: read a short vignette and note resonances or tensions.
0:40-0:55 Closing round: commitments for classroom care and support needs.
0:55-1:00 Quiet writing and thank you.

MATERIALS
Printed consent script
Story vignettes with glossary terms
Exit ticket with support resources
