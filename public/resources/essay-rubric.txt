Short Essay Rubric Rewarding Careful Sources

60-SECOND OVERVIEW
Evaluate short essays with an emphasis on synthesis, precise language, and ethical source use.

CRITERIA
Thesis and through line: clear argument that responds to the prompt.
Evidence and citation: sources integrated with signal phrases and context.
Analysis and connection: explains how evidence advances the claim.
Voice and mechanics: tone stays warm, careful, and proofread.

BANDS
4 advanced: exceeds expectations with layered insight and polished prose.
3 proficient: meets expectations with room for deeper connection.
2 developing: needs guidance on structure or citation.
1 emerging: schedule a revision conference and share models.
