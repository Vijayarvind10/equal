Privacy & ethics

Consent-first practices, no surprises

We only keep what we need to run this site and deliver resources. Every story, download, and workshop operates with transparent consent and easy exits.

  1. We collect only what we need. This includes contact information you provide, workshop logistics, and story consent logs.
  2. We never sell or share your data. Email addresses and stories stay in secured, access-limited folders.
  3. You can request deletion at any time. Email hello@equalvoicescollective.org with "Delete my information" in the subject line. We confirm completion within 24 hours.
  4. Contact us if something feels off. We document and respond to every concern quickly and transparently.

We also maintain a private log that shows when stories change status so you can trust that your preferences are honored.
