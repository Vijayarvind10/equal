How a NeighborLift circuit runs

Launch a team in less than a week with our plug-and-play plan.

Step 1 - Gather

Use the interest form and volunteer agreements to build a roster and confirm availability.

Step 2 - Match

Pair volunteers with residents using the scheduling matrix and safety buddy system.

Step 3 - Support

Track every visit, log small needs, and escalate concerns through the on-call coordinator list.
