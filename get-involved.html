What happens after you sign up

  1. We book a 20-minute onboarding call to learn your strengths and schedule.
  2. You receive a tailored welcome pack with scripts, checklists, and contact lists.
  3. We match you with a circuit and provide ongoing support through group messages and office hours.

Questions? Email hello@neighborlift.org or call the support line listed on the contact page.
